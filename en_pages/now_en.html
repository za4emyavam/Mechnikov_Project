At the moment

    Today, ONU is a corps of more than ten thousand students and one and a half thousand students. Among the latter are more than 20 academicians and corresponding members of state and branch academies, 18 honored figures of science, education, culture, 12 laureates of state prizes and other state prizes, almost 120 doctors of sciences, professors, more than 600 candidates of sciences, associate professors working at almost 100 departments. Every year the university holds several dozen international conferences, publishes several hundred monographs, textbooks and manuals and thousands of scientific articles.

The main building of the Odessa National University I.I. Mechnikov.

Physical and chemical building of the University named after I.I. Mechnikov.

    The university trains specialists on the basis of the relevant license in 20 areas and more than 40 specialties. On May 13, 2020, Odessa II Mechnikov National University celebrated its 155th anniversary.
